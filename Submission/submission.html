Book Submission

Please fill out the form below to submit your manuscript to our publishing firm.

Submission Instructions

  1. Author Name: Enter your full legal name. This will be used for official correspondence.
  2. Author Email: Provide a valid email address. We will use this to communicate with you regarding your submission.
  3. Book Title: Enter the title of your book. Make sure it matches the title on your manuscript.
  4. Book Genre: Specify the genre of your book (e.g., Fiction, Non-Fiction, Fantasy, etc.).
  5. Synopsis: Provide a brief summary of your book. This should be a concise overview of the main plot points and characters.
  6. Manuscript: Upload your manuscript as a PDF file. Ensure that the file is formatted correctly and follows our submission guidelines.
